## Why Shugur Relay?

Shugur Relay is designed for operators who need reliability, observability, and horizontal scale in their Nostr infrastructure.

  <Card title="Enterprise Grade" icon="star">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Scalable" icon="puzzle">
